FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Starter projects typically take 4–6 weeks. Growth projects run 8–16 weeks. Enterprise timelines are scoped per engagement. We set realistic deadlines and stick to them.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We have clients across Africa, Europe, and North America. We work async-first with overlap windows for real-time collaboration.

What's included in post-launch support?

Bug fixes, minor content updates, performance monitoring, and server health checks. Larger feature additions are scoped as separate engagements.

What payment terms do you offer?

We typically work on a 40/40/20 split: 40% upfront, 40% at midpoint milestone, 20% on delivery. Enterprise contracts have custom payment schedules.
